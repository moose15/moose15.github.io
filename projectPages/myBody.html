My Body

Role: Designer, 3D Artist | Date Developed: Fall 2015

My Body is a narrative experience about a teenager and an alien sharing the same body. It was a group project created for my Design for Interactive Media and Experiments in Interactivity courses. In addition to helping design this project, I also created the 3D models for it. I used a mix of Maya , Mudbox and Photoshop to model, sculpt and texture a total of fourteen small models in about a week and a half. This project was created with the Unity Game Engine.
