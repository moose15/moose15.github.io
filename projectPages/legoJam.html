Building Memories with Lego

Role: Technical Designer
Software: Unity Game Engine | Language: C#
Date Developed: June 2018

Building Memories with Lego is an augmented reality concept design built over a week long period for the 2018 Lego + USC AR Game Jam. The concept revolves around exploration, with players unlocking various lego models to view in AR and building a virtual lego diorama of visited landmarks. It is targeted toward families, tourists, and all-around lego fans.

As technical designer on the project, I programmed the demo and designed the demo's user interface in addition to helping design the overall concept. Team Brick Chicks consisted of Gabriela Purri R. Gomes (Visual Artist & Film Editor) and Stephanie Henderson (Writer).
