Invasion

Role: All | Date Developed: Spring 2014 | Language: JavaScript, HTML5, CSS3

Invasion is a typing game where you must defend your homeland from the invading vikings. In order to stop an enemy, all the player has to do is type in the word above the enemy's head. Then the enemy will explode. The more enemies the player kills, the more that will spawn. If nine vikings get to the bottom of the canvas, the player loses.

I created this project for my Casual Games class at RIT.

Play the game here: Play Invasion! Note: Vikings may not drop from the top of the canvas right away.
