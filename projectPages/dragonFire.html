Dragon Fire

Role: All | Date Developed: Fall 2012 | Language: ActionScript 3.0

Dragon Fire is a dragon themed flash game in which the player uses their mouse to control a dragon and the space bar to shoot fire balls at enemies flying across the screen. The number of enemies increases with each new level. The player must survive all levels in order to beat the game.

I created this project for my interactive digital media class.
