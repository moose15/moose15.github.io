So You Want to be A Barista?

Role: All | Date Developed: Spring 2014 | Language: JavaScript, HTML5, CSS3

I don't know about you, but I like knowing how they make those fancy coffee drinks at my favorite coffee shop. Therefore, I created this game for my Casual Games course to learn how.

The premise is simple; make as many drinks possible before time runs out or too many orders are unfilled. Orders will appear on the left side of the screen. The player may fill them by clicking the ingredients on the bottom of the canvas and then clicking "Order up!".
