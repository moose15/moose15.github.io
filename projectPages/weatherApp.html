Weather Web App

Role: All | Date Developed: Fall 2013 | Language: JavaScript, HTML5, CSS3

A weather web app that shows the current weather condition of any city in the world. It features various canvas animations including rain, snow, clouds, and thunderstorm. It also features a Google Maps.

APIs used: openweathermap.org, Google Maps
