Stepstone Island

Exercise to collect resources, clean debris, and nurture Stepstone Island back to health! - Coming soon to the Apple App Store.

About

Help Olly the Osprey nurture Stepstone Island back to health in this mobile fitness game! Using the health data collected by the Health app on your iPhone, your step count and distance walked will be converted into coins and milestones. These currencies can then be used to purchase items in Olly's Outlet such as tools, plants, and landmarks. Depending on what you place onto the island, guests may visit the island and support your hard work!


Feel free to follow @StepstoneIsland on twitter for updates on the project!
