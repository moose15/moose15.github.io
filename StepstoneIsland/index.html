Game Title

The Island is a mobile game where players clean up and nurture their own virtual island through exercise. The app takes in real data from fitness trackers such as FitBit or Apple’s Healthkit App and converts it into in-game currency. This currency may be used to clean debris, plant trees, build landmarks, and interact with the game-world. The more beautiful and clean your island becomes, the higher the chance of characters visiting your island and supporting your hard work!
