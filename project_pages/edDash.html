Ed Dashboard Prototype
Role: UX Designer | Software: Axure RP 8 | Date Developed: Fall 2017
The Emergency Department Dashboard Prototype is a part of a larger ongoing project aimed at improving the quality and value of healthcare delivery through a process of audit and feedback (A&F). A&F is a process by which healthcare providers are given data of their own performance in order to improve their healthcare practices and delivery.
This project is lead by Dr. Welmoed Van Deen in association with the USC Gehr Family Center for Health Systems Science. In fall 2017, I was hired to create a performance dashboard prototype for emergency department personnel. I also designed an email report associated with the dashboard (see image below). The prototype was created in Axure RP 8 over the course of 15 weeks (approximately 80 hours total).
