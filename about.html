About

Hello there! My name is Katie and I am a game designer. My personal projects tend to lean toward a cute, quirky, and at times absurdly humorous style. If I can make a person smile, laugh, and learn with my projects, then I consider that a job well done.

In 2015, I graduated from the Rochester Institute of Technology in Rochester, New York with a BSc in Game Design & Development. I then moved directly to Los Angeles, California (talk about a drastic change in climate) where I acquired a MFA in Interactive Media at the University of Southern California in 2018. I currently reside in Los Angeles where I cherish cool, cloudy, and rainy days.
