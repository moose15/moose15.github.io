Who am I?

I'm a game designer, interaction designer, and gameplay programmer interested in creating fun and engaging interactive experiences that benefit people in a positive way. I graduated from the Rochester Institute of Technology in 2015 with a BSc in Game Design and Development and then obtained a MFA in Interactive Media at the University of Southern California in 2018. I currently reside in Los Angeles, CA.
